We bring a single, branded AI workspace to your organization. Inside it, specialized agents take on the repetitive, structured work that clogs your front line—so your people can focus on matters, patients, clients, and growth.

System 01

AI intake & triage

Capture the right details on every call, form, or message. Route by practice area, service type, urgency, and fit—using criteria you define and approve.

System 02
